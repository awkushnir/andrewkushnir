The Division

A gripping new work on identity, legacy, and the slyness of memory — a 19,000-kilometre journey through Ukraine and Europe in the shadow of an inherited timepiece and a family mythology. Written and directed by Andrew Kushnir; a Project: Humanity and Pyretic Productions production in association with Crow's Theatre.

Recognition

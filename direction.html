Work

Direction

My directing practice has become central to my evolving work in the theatre. I've been profoundly influenced by the directors who've undertaken my new plays, and by the methodologies of biggies like Katie Mitchell and Declan Donnellan. I'm fascinated by how one develops a "contract of care" between artists, and between artists and their audience.

Publication

When it comes to ink, I've been privileged to have plays published, to contribute chapters to academic texts, and to pen articles for newspapers and digital platforms — alongside writing about my practice authored by others.

  • Global Youth Citizenry and Radical Hope — Springer, chapter (2019)

  • In Defence of Theatre: Aesthetic Practices and Social Interventions — University of Toronto Press, chapter (2016)
